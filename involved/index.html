Financial Stewardship

How Your Support Is Used

Every contribution directly funds the tools and logistics needed to bring free screenings to communities in need.

Screening Equipment

Validated digital blood pressure monitors, Android devices for community-level data collection, and supplies for each screening event.

Training & Curriculum

Training of Trainers programs, community volunteer training sessions, and printed educational materials in English and local languages.

Follow-Up & Data

Phone credit for twelve-month follow-up calls, data management through GHSI's tracking system, and M&E reporting.

Ways to Get Involved

Whoever you are, there is a place for you in this work.

Lend your skills

We welcome practitioners with relevant experience in:

  • Clinical care (cardiology, internal medicine, family medicine, nursing)
  • Public health, epidemiology, or implementation research
  • Community engagement and health communication
  • Design, data systems, or digital health (DHIMS-2 integration, Ghana-based builders)
  • Communications, content, and donor outreach

All current roles are volunteer-stage. We are transparent about this on first contact.

Build with us

We partner with implementation, research, religious, and community-based organizations on:

  • Adopting or adapting the GHSI volunteer training curriculum for other Ghanaian regions
  • Joining the December 2026 pilot as a screening or referral site
  • Research collaboration on community-level hypertension screening and twelve-month follow-up
  • Faith-based health partnership across Greater Accra
