Working Communities at Risk

Healthcare Access Gaps

Our Approach

A community-based screening model designed for real-world impact.

01

Meet People Where They Are

We conduct screenings at trotro stations and market centers during work hours, eliminating barriers of time and travel. No appointments needed.

02

Trusted Community Partnerships

We work with GPRTU station masters, Market Queens, and community leaders who already have the trust of the people we serve.

03

Screen & Refer

We focus on screening and education, then connect those with elevated readings to local healthcare providers for follow-up care.

04

Data-Driven Impact

Every screening is documented to track community health patterns, measure impact, and improve our approach over time.

About GHSI

The Ghana Hypertension Screening Initiative is a community-based health infrastructure project designed to bring preventive cardiovascular screening to populations that formal healthcare systems often miss.

Founded on the principles of community partnership and sustainable health systems, GHSI is not charity‚Äîit's health infrastructure development. We work with existing community structures to create screening capacity that can be maintained and expanded over time.

Our model is designed for replication. What begins as a pilot can become a template for addressing preventive health gaps across Ghana and beyond.
